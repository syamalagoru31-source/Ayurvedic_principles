  • Steamed vegetables
  • Buttermilk with roasted cumin

  • Fresh fruit or dry fruits

  • Homemade snacks
7:00 PM

Dinner

  • Light soup or vegetable stew
  • Warm milk with turmeric
  • Early dinner (before 8 PM)

Universal Morning Rituals for All Ages

Oil Pulling

Swish 1 tbsp sesame oil in mouth for 10-15 minutes. Removes toxins and improves oral health.

Tongue Scraping

Clean tongue with copper scraper to remove toxins and improve digestion.

Pranayama

5-10 minutes of deep breathing exercises to oxygenate blood and calm mind.

Meditation

5-15 minutes of mindfulness to reduce stress and improve focus.
