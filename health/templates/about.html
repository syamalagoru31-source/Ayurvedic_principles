What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient Indian system of medicine that focuses on prevention rather than cure. It emphasizes diet, lifestyle, herbal remedies, and balance of the three doshas — Vata, Pitta, and Kapha — for optimal health and longevity.
