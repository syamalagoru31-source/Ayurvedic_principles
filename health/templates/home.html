What is Preventive Healthcare?

Understanding the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda

Preventive healthcare in Ayurveda focuses on maintaining balance between Vata, Pitta, and Kapha through proper diet, lifestyle, and natural remedies. It's not just about treating illness, but about creating an environment of wellness that prevents disease from taking hold.

Ayurvedic Diet Recommendation

Personalized nutrition based on your dosha type

Vata

Warm, cooked foods, rice, milk, ghee, nuts, and sweet fruits. Avoid cold and dry foods.

Pitta

Cooling foods like cucumber, coconut water, sweet fruits, and leafy greens. Avoid spicy foods.

Kapha

Light, warm foods like barley, vegetables, legumes, and honey. Avoid heavy, oily foods.
